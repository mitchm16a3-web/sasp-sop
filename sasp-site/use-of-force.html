Use of Force

Troopers must use only the force necessary to safely control a situation.

Force Levels

Officers must escalate and de-escalate appropriately.

Taser Use

Only used on actively resisting subjects.

Firearm Use

Lethal force is a last resort and must be justified.

Class Matching

Use appropriate force based on suspect threat level.
