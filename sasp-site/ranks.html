Rank Structure

The San Andreas State Police follows a clear chain of command to maintain professionalism, accountability, and operational efficiency.

Cadet

Entry-level position. Must complete FTO training.

Trooper

Full patrol authority. Expected to know SOPs and operate independently.

Senior Trooper

Experienced trooper trusted with mentoring and scene leadership.

Corporal

First-line supervisor. Assists with training and oversight.

Sergeant

Supervises patrol operations and ensures SOP compliance.

Lieutenant

Manages divisions, oversees supervisors, handles administrative duties.

Captain

High-level command. Oversees department-wide operations.

Major

Executive command. Handles policy, structure, and department direction.

Colonel

Head of the SASP. Final authority on all operations and SOPs.
