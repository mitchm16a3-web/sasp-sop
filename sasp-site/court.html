Court Procedures

Troopers may be required to testify or provide documentation for court cases. Accuracy and professionalism are essential.

Testimony

Troopers must provide truthful, clear, and consistent statements.

Evidence

All evidence must be properly logged and preserved.

Reports

Incident and arrest reports must be complete and accurate.

Subpoenas

Troopers must appear in court when subpoenaed unless excused by command.
