Equipment

Troopers must use only authorized equipment and ensure all gear is maintained and deployed properly.

Standard Issue

Includes duty pistol, taser, radio, handcuffs, and body armor.

Long Guns

Shotguns and rifles require certification and supervisor approval.

Specialized Gear

Spike strips, breaching tools, and tactical equipment require training.

Maintenance

Troopers must ensure all equipment is functional before and after patrol.
