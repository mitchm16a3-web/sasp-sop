Discipline & Conduct

All SASP personnel are expected to maintain professionalism, integrity, and respect at all times.

Professionalism

Troopers must treat civilians and officers with respect and maintain composure.

Chain of Command

Orders must be followed unless unlawful or unsafe.

Violations

Includes abuse of power, negligence, harassment, or SOP breaches.

Disciplinary Actions

Can include warnings, suspensions, demotions, or termination.
