Pursuits

Pursuits must be conducted safely, professionally, and within department policy.

PIT Authorization

PIT may only be used when authorized by a supervisor or when deadly force is justified.

Tracking

Units must call out direction, speed, and traffic conditions.

Air Support

When active, ground units should reduce risk and maintain distance.

Termination

Pursuits must be terminated if risk outweighs necessity.
